How HarvestLink Works

From your farm in Aurora to a buyer's kitchen in Metro Manila — in four simple steps.

🌱
Register Your Farm

Sign up free via app or SMS. Verify your farm location in Baler, Casiguran, Dipaculao, or any of Aurora's 8 municipalities.

📦
List Your Produce

Upload photos, set your price, and publish your harvest. Reach verified buyers in Metro Manila, Cabanatuan, and beyond instantly.

🤝
Receive Orders

Get notified via app or SMS. Confirm orders, coordinate pickup with our logistics partners, and prepare your goods.

💸
Get Paid Instantly

Payment via GCash or bank transfer within 24 hours of delivery confirmation. No waiting, no haggling, no middlemen.
